About Our Mission

Build A Space Program was created to share knowledge, experience, and insights about the space industry, rocketry, and spacecraft engineering. Our mission is to make space knowledge accessible to everyone, from students and hobbyists to professionals seeking new perspectives.

Why We Started This Blog

The path to becoming a space engineer is often unclear and filled with challenges. We started this blog to document "Things I wish I knew when I was starting out as a space engineer" - practical knowledge that can help newcomers navigate this exciting field.

What We Cover

  • Launch vehicle technologies and developments
  • Spacecraft systems and engineering challenges
  • Space mission planning and execution
  • Industry trends and opportunities
  • Educational resources and career guidance
